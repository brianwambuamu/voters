Joseph Ole Lenku
Innovation for Prosperity

Vision: To transform our county into a beacon of modern governance and economic opportunity through strategic investment in technology and sustainable development.
Healthcare: We will establish digital health records systems across all county hospitals and clinics to improve patient management and service delivery. Our focus will be on preventative care programs, leveraging mobile clinics and telemedicine to reach remote areas and reduce the burden on major facilities.
Security: We commit to enhancing security through a community policing model integrated with modern surveillance technology. We will invest in advanced rapid response units and collaborate closely with national security agencies, while also empowering local peace committees.
Youth Employment: We will launch County Tech Hubs and Innovation Centers to equip our youth with digital skills, coding expertise, and entrepreneurial mentorship. Our administration will facilitate partnerships with the private sector to create internship programs and provide seed funding for youth-led startups.



David Nkedianye
Community First, Sustainable Growth

Vision: To build a resilient and inclusive county where every citizen thrives, by prioritizing grassroots development and empowering local communities to drive their own progress.
Healthcare: We will strengthen community health worker networks and decentralize healthcare services by establishing well-equipped dispensaries in every sub-location. Our focus is on affordable, accessible primary healthcare, ensuring essential medicines are always available at the local level.
Security: We will empower and support local Nyumba Kumi initiatives and community vigilance groups, providing them with necessary resources and training. Our approach emphasizes dialogue, conflict resolution, and fostering strong relationships between citizens and local law enforcement.
Water: We pledge to invest in community-managed boreholes, rainwater harvesting projects, and rehabilitation of natural water sources to ensure sustainable and equitable water access. We will support local water committees to maintain infrastructure and manage resources effectively, reducing reliance on distant supplies.
