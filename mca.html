Delivering Development, One Ward at a Time

Vision: To bring tangible development and improved essential services directly to every household in [Specific Ward Name], ensuring our community thrives through responsive and efficient leadership.
Ward Infrastructure Upgrade: I will prioritize the rehabilitation and construction of ward roads, footpaths, and drainage systems through the Ward Development Fund (WDF) and relentless lobbying of the County Executive. Enhanced Waste Management & Sanitation: I will advocate for improved and regular garbage collection services within our ward, ensuring designated collection points and supporting community initiatives for cleaner environments. Through county by-laws, I will push for strict enforcement of sanitation standards in public spaces and local markets.
Community Facilities & Recreation: I will utilize the WDF to renovate and equip our community halls, youth polytechnics, and local sports grounds, providing safe and functional spaces for community gatherings, skills development, and recreational activities for all age groups.

Your Voice, Our Ward's Future: Accountability and Empowerment

Vision: To empower the residents of [Specific Ward Name] by ensuring transparency in governance, fostering robust public participation, and championing policies that directly improve quality of life at the grassroots.
Transparent Ward Fund Management & Public Participation: I commit to establishing a transparent and inclusive process for WDF allocation, ensuring that community members directly propose, prioritize, and oversee projects in our ward. I will hold regular, accessible public forums (barazas) to account for all funds received and spent.
Strengthening Local Safety & Security: I will work closely with community policing committees, chiefs, and local security agencies to strengthen community watch initiatives and advocate for improved police presence and response times within our ward. Through WDF, I will support initiatives like street lighting and security patrols in identified hotspots.
Responsive County By-Laws: I will actively participate in the County Assembly to propose and support by-laws that address specific local challenges in our ward, such as regulation of informal businesses, noise pollution, and access to clean water. My focus will be on legislation that directly impacts the daily lives and well-being of our residents.
