About Us

Hey There!

CivicVoiceKe System is designed to empower citizens with accurate, timely, and comprehensive information regarding local elections in Kajiado county. As voter apathy and miscommunication contribute to low civic engagement, the systems seek to provide a centralized digital platform where users can access verified data, candidate details, polling locations and voter education materials. By integrating real-time updates, interactive maps and user feedback mechanisms, the platform aims to enhance electoral participation and community awareness. Additionally, the system will support multilingual interfaces to serve diverse communities, ensuring accessibility and inclusiveness. This proposal outlines the background, design, objectives, and work plan for implementing the Local Election Awareness System at a local government level
