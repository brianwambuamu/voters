Leah sankaire
Empowering Every Woman, Unleashing Youth Potential

Vision: To champion transformative policies and programs that empower women economically, protect our most vulnerable, and create a future of limitless opportunity for the youth of our county.
Economic Empowerment & Financial Inclusion: I will leverage the NGAAF to establish county-wide entrepreneurship hubs that provide accessible credit, business training, and market linkages for women and youth-led enterprises. My legislative agenda will include advocating for policies that break down barriers to economic participation for women, ensuring they have equitable access to resources and opportunities
Combatting Gender-Based Violence (GBV) & Promoting Safety: I am committed to fighting GBV through strengthened legislative frameworks and increased funding for safe houses, counseling services, and survivor support networks across our county. I will lead public awareness campaigns to shift societal attitudes and collaborate with law enforcement to ensure perpetrators face justice.
Health & Well-being for Women and Girls: My advocacy in Parliament will focus on securing increased national budget allocation for reproductive health services, maternal care, and adolescent health programs within our county. I will champion initiatives that raise awareness about critical health issues and ensure health facilities are sensitive to the unique needs of women and girls.



Janet Marania
Voice for the Voiceless, Architect of Inclusion

Vision: To build an inclusive society where every individual, regardless of gender or ability, has equal access to justice, opportunities, and a dignified life, through robust advocacy and strategic resource allocation.
Advancing Social Inclusion & Rights: I will actively push for legislation that strengthens the rights and participation of Persons With Disabilities (PWDs), ensuring accessible public infrastructure, equal employment opportunities, and specialized support services. Through NGAAF, I will fund programs that provide assistive devices and vocational training tailored for PWDs.
Capacity Building & Leadership Development: I will establish county-wide mentorship and training programs targeting women and youth to equip them with leadership skills, political literacy, and confidence to participate effectively in governance and decision-making processes, from community level to national politics.
Innovation & Digital Literacy for Empowerment: I will leverage NGAAF to establish digital literacy hubs in every sub-county, providing free training in basic computer skills, online entrepreneurship, and digital safety. My goal is to bridge the digital divide and empower women and youth to harness technology for economic gain and informed civic participation.
