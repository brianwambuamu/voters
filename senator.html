Voice of the County, Architect of Sustainable Governance

Vision: To strategically represent our county in the Senate, fostering collaboration, driving innovative legislation, and ensuring our county's unique challenges and opportunities are addressed effectively at the national stage.
Legislative Excellence for County Empowerment: I will actively participate in the passage of Bills that directly empower county governments to deliver essential services more efficiently, focusing on legislation that streamlines procurement, enhances local revenue collection mechanisms, and supports climate resilience initiatives at the county level.
Fostering Inter-County Collaboration & Regional Development: I will champion initiatives in the Senate that encourage inter-county partnerships for shared development goals, such as joint infrastructure projects, tourism circuits, or agricultural value chains. Enhancing Public Participation in County Governance: I will advocate for stronger national frameworks that mandate genuine public participation in county decision-making processes, from budget formulation to development planning. My oversight will ensure our county government provides accessible platforms for citizens to voice their needs and hold leaders accountable.
Resolution of Inter-Governmental Disputes & Policy Harmony: I will utilize the Senate's role to mediate and resolve disputes between our county government and national government entities, ensuring smooth implementation of devolution. My legislative work will also focus on ensuring national policies are harmonized with county development plans, avoiding conflicts and promoting synergistic growth.



Mary Seneta
Championing Devolution, Ensuring County Prosperity

Vision: To be a vigilant guardian of our county's interests in the Senate, fighting for increased resources and robust oversight to ensure that devolution truly transforms lives at the grassroots.
Fair & Increased County Revenue Allocation: I will advocate relentlessly for a just and increased share of national revenue to our county, ensuring that the formula for allocation genuinely reflects our population, poverty index, and unique geographical needs. My legislative focus will be on strengthening the legal framework for revenue sharing to prevent arbitrary cuts or delays.
Strict Oversight & Accountability for County Funds: I commit to rigorous oversight of all national funds allocated to our county government, ensuring every shilling is spent transparently and achieves its intended impact. I will demand regular reports, conduct thorough investigations into misuse of funds, and advocate for strong penalties for corruption to protect taxpayer money.
Protecting County Assets & Resources: I will tirelessly work to protect our county's land, natural resources, and strategic assets from national encroachment or mismanagement. Through legislation and oversight, I will ensure that any national policies affecting our resources are consultative, fair, and primarily benefit the residents of our county.
